About Me

Hi! Welcome to Fan Yang's homepage. I am a third-year CSE PhD student at The Ohio State University (OSU), under the supervision of Prof. John Paparrizos. Prior to the academic journey at OSU, I obtained M.S. degree at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU) in 2023, advised by Prof. Bhiksha Raj, and received B.S. degree from Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (NJUPT) in 2020. My research focuses on Data Mining, Machine Learning, Multimodal Learning (e.g., Computer Vision, Speech Recognition, Time-series Analysis). Ask me anything if you are interested!

I am actively conducting research in time-series domains, with a broad interest including but not limited to classification, clustering, anomaly detection and forecasting. If you are interested in my work, please feel free to contact me via email: yang.7007@osu.edu.
